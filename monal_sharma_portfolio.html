About the Artist

Painting the world
one masterpiece at a time

Monal Sharma is a celebrated Indian artist whose work transcends the boundaries of traditional art forms. With a rare mastery over sketching, abstract painting, realistic portraiture, and the meditative intricacy of mandala art, Monal has carved a singular identity in the world of fine arts.

From intimate pencil sketches that capture the soul of a subject, to vibrant abstract compositions that dance with colour and emotion, every piece by Monal is a testament to years of disciplined practice and boundless creative passion.

Her dedication to her craft has earned her recognition not only across India but on the world stage — with her iconic rendering of Jaipur's Hawa Mahal being exhibited at the prestigious Louvre Museum, Paris, as a work of cultural heritage.
